第25天 主谓一致

25-1 主谓一致的原则

“一致”是指句子成分之间或者词语之间在语法形式上的协调关系。主谓一致指主语和谓语动词之间在“人称”和“数”的方面的一致关系。
主谓一致的问题看似简单，实际使用时却很复杂，因为不同情况下处理这一问题所依据的原则各不相同。现代语法中，主谓一致通常有三种基本原则：
1. 语法一致（grammatical concord）
主语和谓语从语法形式上取得一致，主语是单数形式，谓语也用单数；主语是复数形式，谓语用复数。
例：We have our own merits. Each of us has merits.

  • 不定式、动名词、从句作主语时看作单数，谓语用单数。
    Reading books is a very good way to study.
    To do this kind of thing requires courage.
    What you have done has been seen.
  • 不定代词one, every, each, everybody, everyone, one of, no one, nothing, nobody, someone, somebody, either, neither, many a等作主语或修饰主语时看作单数，谓语用单数。
    Neither of them is guilty.
    Many a student is doing exercise.
  • 专有名词作主语时看作单数，谓语用单数。
    Gone With the Wind is a book known to lovers of English.
  • a portion, a series of, a kind of, the number of等作主语时看作单数，谓语用单数。
    A kind of flower in the garden smells very pleasant.
    A series of expensive goods has been sold out in the shop.
  • some, several, both, few, many, a number of等修饰主语或自身作主语时看作复数，谓语用复数。and连接两个主语时，谓语一般用复数。
    Both of us are basketball players.
    A number of people have applied for the job.
  • a lot of, most of, any of, half of, three fifths of, eighty percent of, some of, none of, the rest of, all of等后接不可数名词或单数名词作主语时用单数，接可数名词复数时用复数。
    A lot of books have been published recently.
    A lot of money in the shop was stolen last night.
2. 意义一致（notional concord）
主语形式为单数但意义为复数时，谓语用复数；主语形式为复数但意义为单数时，谓语用单数。
  • 主语后接as well as, as much as, accompanied by, including, in addition to, more than, no less than, rather than, together with等词组时，谓语形式依主语单复数而定。
    Food, along with vegetable, has recently risen in price.
    The teacher, with all his students, is going to have a picnic this weekend.
  • 表示时间、金钱、距离、体积、重量、面积、数字等词作主语，若指总量用单数，若指数量用复数。
    Seven days are often regarded as one week.
    Ten years stands for a long period in my life.
  • the + 形容词作主语，指个人或抽象概念用单数，指一类人用复数。
    The unknown is always something to be feared.
    The accused was/were acquitted of the charge.
    In our society, the good are rewarded.
  • and连接两个主语指同一人、物、事、概念时用单数，指不同事物时用复数。
    Love and right is a constant theme in history.
    This shirt and that shirt are of good quality.
  • 集体名词作主语，表示整体时用单数，表示成员时用复数。cattle, people, police一般看作复数。
    The class are all fond of basketball.
    The family is quite successful.
    The public has right to vote for the president.
    The public now come to know that they were cheated by the government.
3. 就近原则（principle of proximity）
谓语动词的人称和数常与距离最近的主语保持一致。

25-2 主谓一致主要方法总结

  • 并列结构作主语时的主谓一致
    • and连接两个单数名词作主语时，谓语一般用复数；若指同一人、事、概念时用单数。
      A boy and a girl want to go to church.
      A bowl and chopsticks was set on the table.
    • 一个单数名词被两个不同形容词修饰，表示不同概念时谓语用复数，表示同一概念时用单数。
      White and black coffee are both we like to drink.
      The last and most important thing is to have a meal.
    • each... and each..., every... and every..., no... and no..., many a... and many a...结构作主语时，谓语用单数。
      No boy and no girl is to be found in the classroom.
      Many a teacher and many a student is busy with the exam.
    • not only... but also..., either...or..., neither... nor..., ...or...连接的并列主语，谓语依就近一致原则。
      Either the students or the teacher is responsible for the case.
    • 主语后接as well as, along with, together with, rather than, no less than, but, except, besides, in addition to, like, including等词组时，谓语单复数由前面的词决定。
      A school, with some houses, is to be built here.
      Nancy, along with her friends, goes shopping every Sunday.
  • 百分数、分数作主语时的主谓一致
    百分数、分数后加名词或代词，谓语单复数由该名词或代词决定。
    Three-fourths of the surface of the earth is sea.
    Fifty percent of the students in our school are girls.
  • 不定代词作主语时的主谓一致
    • each, another, the other, either, neither和由some, any, no, every等加one或thing构成的复合代词作主语时，谓语用单数。
      Nobody likes here.
      Neither of us is allergic to it.
      Something has been done to help the poor.
    • none作主语时，谓语可用单数也可用复数。
      None of us like his present.
      None of us has ever been there.
    • both, (a) few, many, several作主语时，谓语用复数。
      Both of them like singing.
    • all作主语表示人时谓语用复数，表示物时用单数。
      All is clear.
      All are here.
  • 表示"全体" "部分"等意义的词作主语时的主谓一致
    • 主语是most, the rest, the last, the remainder等时，谓语依意义一致原则。of后名词单数用单数，复数用复数。
      Half of the students do this, the rest are to do that.
      After the big fire, the remainder is nothing.
    • town, school, village等表示总称时，谓语可用单数也可用复数。可用the whole修饰。
      The whole town is / are celebrating the big event.
