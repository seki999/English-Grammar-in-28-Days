第22天 定语从句

22-1 定语从句的概念

在句中起定语作用，修饰某一名词或代词，或修饰整个主句的从句，叫做定语从句 (the attributive clause)。被定语从句修饰的词叫先行词 (antecedent)。定语从句通常跟在先行词之后，由“关系词 (relatives)”引出，因此定语从句又可称为关系分句。
关系词可分为：
关系代词： which, that, who, whom, whose 等。
关系副词： when, where, why 等。
关系词常有3个作用：1）引导定语从句；2）代替先行词；3）在定语从句中担当一个成分（如主语、宾语、定语和状语等）。
例：The girl who is singing in the classroom is my sister.
（the girl是先行词，who引导定语从句并在从句中作主语）
This is the room where Lu Xun once lived.
（the room是先行词，where引导定语从句并作地点状语）

22-2 关系代词引导的定语从句

关系代词所代替的先行词是表示人或物的名词或代词，并在从句中充当主语、宾语、定语等成分。关系代词在定语从句中作主语时，从句谓语动词的人称和数要和先行词保持一致。

  • who, whom, that：代替人的名词或代词。who作主语，whom作宾语，that可作主语或宾语（作宾语可省略），可指人也可指物。
    The boys who are playing football are from Class One.
    She is the girl whom/that I saw yesterday.
    The number of the people that/who came to China each year rises one million.
  • whose：指人或物，指物时可与of which互换，指人时也可用of whom。
    I have a classmate whose (of whom) father is a doctor.
    Please give me the pencil whose (of which) color is green.
  • which, that：代替事物的名词或代词，在从句中可作主语、宾语等，作宾语时可省略。
    Computer game is something which is liked by most boys.
    Where is the book that I read this morning?

22-3 关系副词引导的定语从句

常见的关系副词有：why = for which，where = in/at/on/... which，when = during/on/in/... which。

  • when 指时间，在定语从句中作时间状语。
    The time when we got together finally came.
  • where 指地点，在定语从句中作地点状语。
    Beijing is the city where I was born.
  • why 指原因，在定语从句中作原因状语。
    Please tell me the reason why you are late.

22-4 介词+关系代词引导的定语从句

关系代词在定语从句中作介词宾语时，从句常由“介词 + 关系代词”引导。
This is the school in which I studied two years ago.
We'll go to hear the famous singer about whom we have often talked.

22-5 限定性定语从句和非限定性定语从句

  • 1. 限定性定语从句
    China is a country which has a long history.
  • 2. 非限定性定语从句
    His mother, who loves him very much, is strict with him.

关系代词在定语从句中作介词宾语时，从句常由“介词 + 关系代词”引导。
This is the school in which I studied two years ago.
We'll go to hear the famous singer about whom we have often talked.

  • 3. 难点分析
    • 只能用that的几种情况：
      1. 先行词为anything, everything, nothing, few, all, none, little, some等代词，或由every, any, all, some, no, little, few, much, each等修饰时，只能用that（有时可省略）。
        Have you taken down everything (that) Mr. Lee has said?
        All that can be done has been done.
        There is little (that) I can do for you.
        Any man that/who has a sense of duty won't do such a thing.
      2. 先行词前有who, which等疑问代词时，只能用that。
        Who is the man that is standing there?
        Which is the T-shirt that fits me most?
      3. 先行词被最高级修饰时，只能用that。
        This is the best film that I have seen.
      4. 先行词被the very, the only, the same, the last修饰时，只能用that。
        This is the very good dictionary that I want to buy.
        After the fire in his house, the old car is the only thing that he owned.
      5. 先行词被序数词修饰时，只能用that。
        The first place that they visited in London was the Big Ben.
      6. 关系代词在定语从句中做表语时，只能用that。
        The village is no longer the one that it used to be 10 years ago.
      7. 先行词既有人，也有动物或物体时，只能用that。
        The writer and his works that you told me about are admired by us all.
    • as和which的区别：
      • as和which都可在定语从句中做主语或宾语，代表前面整个句子。
        He married her, as/which was natural.
      • 先行词被such, the same修饰时，常用as。
        I have never heard such a story as he tells.
        This is the same book as I lost last week.
      • as引导非限制性定语从句，可放主句前、后或切割主句；which只能放主句后。as有“正如……”的意思。
        As is known to all, China is a developing country.
        He is from the south, as we can see from his accent.
        John, as you know, is a famous writer.
        He has been to Paris more than several times, which I don't believe.
    • the way为先行词时，常用in which, that引导，且可省略。
      The way in which/that he did things was unacceptable.
    • but有时也可做关系词引导定语从句。
      There are very few but know what he is thinking. (but = who don't)
    • 区分定语从句和同位语从句：
      • 定语从句由关系代词或关系副词引导，关系词在句中充当成分，有时可省略；同位语从句主要由that引导，在句中一般不做成分。
        The problem that we are facing now is how we can collect so much money.（定语从句）
        The problem how we can collect so much money is difficult to solve.（同位语从句）
      • 定语从句修饰先行词，是修饰关系；同位语从句说明先行词的具体内容，是补充说明关系。
        The flight that took off just now is to London.（定语从句）
        The fact that he has been dead is clear.（同位语从句）
      • 同位语从句和先行词一般可用be动词发展成完整句子，定语从句不可以。
        The idea that we could ask the teacher for advice is wonderful.（同位语从句）
        The idea that we have just heard is wonderful.（定语从句）
