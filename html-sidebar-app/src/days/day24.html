第24天 插入语、直接引语和间接引语

24-1 插入语

1. 概念
插入语是插在句子中的一个词、短语或从句，用来补充含义或作解释。插入语通常被逗号、破折号或句子的其他部分隔开，在句中与其他部分没有语法上的联系，删掉后句子结构仍完整。

2. 插入语的类型
插入语可表示说话人的看法、态度、推测、解释、说明、总结、强调、引起注意、转移话题等。常见类型有：

  • 形容词或形容词短语：True, wonderful, worse still, sure enough, strange, most important of all, strange to say 等。
    例：True, it would be too bad. Wonderful, we have won again. Strange, there is nobody in the classroom.
  • 副词或副词短语：indeed, surely, however, frankly, obviously, naturally, fortunately, briefly, honestly, besides, exactly, still, otherwise, luckily 等。
    例：Honestly, I don't need it at the moment. You'll be able to pass the coming exam, surely. Unfortunately, neither of them could swim.
  • 介词短语：in a way, for example, of course, as a matter of fact, by the way, on the contrary, on the other hand, in my opinion, in general, in a sense, in short, in conclusion 等。
    例：In a way, I think we both won in the game. In my opinion, he is an honest boy. In short, we should not stop halfway.
  • 现在分词短语：generally speaking, judging from/by..., talking of..., strictly speaking, considering, putting it mildly 等。
    例：Judging from his face, he must be ill. Generally speaking, dogs can run faster than pigs.
  • 过去分词短语：Painted white, we like the house better.
  • 动词不定式：to be sure, to be frank, to tell you the truth, so to speak, to begin with 等。
    例：To be frank, I don't quite agree with you. To tell you the truth, I am not very interested in going to the show.
  • 代词词组：all the same, all told, all in all 等。
    例：All in all, they have had a wonderful time today.
  • 从句：if so, if not, if any, if I may say so, as you know, as you say, if you like, if you please, if necessary, as it happens 等。
    例：If I may say so, we know nothing about it. This man, as you know, is good for nothing.
  • 简短句子：I am sure, do you know, you see, I'm afraid, it is said, I suppose/think/believe/hope, what's more, what's worse, that is to say, what is important 等。
    例：I believe, China will catch up with the developed countries sooner or later. The old man, it is said, was an artist.
  • 标点符号引导：He was (strange as it seems) an excellent sportsman. He was to me at least, if not to you——a figure that was worth having pity on.

3. 插入语特殊用法
插入语独立性强，一般用标点符号隔开。疑问句中插入成分（如do you think, do you suppose等）常见于口语，疑问句应保持陈述语序。
例：
What do you think I should do first?
Who do you think is singing?
How long did you say she would stay here?
When do you suppose they'll be back?
How old did you think she was?

24-2 直接引语和间接引语

1. 直接引语和间接引语之间的转换

  • 人称变化：
    • 主语为第一人称，变为间接引语时按主句主语变化。
      Lily said, "I must go to work." → Lily said she must go to work.
    • 主语为第二人称，变为间接引语时按主句宾语变化。
      He asked me, "How is your mother now?" → He asked me how my mother was then.
    • 主语为第三人称，通常不变。
      My teacher said, "she is a good student." → My teacher said she was a good student.
  • 时态变化：
    • 一般现在时→一般过去时；现在进行时→过去进行时；现在完成时→过去完成时；现在完成进行时→过去完成进行时。
      He said, "The leaders are quarrelling among themselves." → He said (that) the leaders were quarrelling among themselves.
    • 一般过去时→过去完成时（如强调先后）；过去进行时可变为过去完成时或不变。
      He said, "I didn't recognize you." → He said he hadn't recognized me.
    • 将来时→过去将来时。
      He said, "The food will go off if you don't cook them today." → He said the food would go off if you didn't cook them that day.
    • 如原话动作属于尚未到来的将来，可不变。
      He said, "I'll be waiting for you tomorrow." → He said he'll be waiting for me tomorrow.
  • 时间、地点、指示代词等变化：如now→then, today→that day, here→there, this→that, tomorrow→the next day等。

2. 各种句型由直接引语转变为间接引语的方法

  • 陈述句：引述动词为现在时，间接引语动词不变；为过去时，间接引语动词一般要变。
    He said, "The secretary has turned down the proposal." → He said (that) the secretary had turned down the proposal.
  • 疑问句：
    • 一般疑问句、反意疑问句用if或whether引导。
      "Does he really mean that?" → I wondered whether/if he really meant that.
    • 选择疑问句用whether。
      I asked him, "Will you stay at home or go to a film tonight?" → I asked him whether he would stay at home or go to a film that night.
    • 特殊疑问句用原疑问词引导。
      He asked, "Which chair shall I sit in?" → He asked me which chair he should sit in.
    • 请求、建议等用ask, advise, want + 宾语 + 不定式；suggest + V-ing或that从句；offer + 不定式。
      "Would you buy some books?" → He asked me to buy him some books.
      "Shall we get the tickets first?" → He suggested that we (should) get the tickets first.
      "Shall I post them to you?" → He offered to post them to me.
  • 祈使句：
    • 多用“动词 + 宾语 + 不定式”结构。
      "Be careful with the dog." → He warned me to be careful with the dog.
    • 命令句可用“动词 + that分句”结构。
      "Don't run too fast." → He told me that I wasn't to run too fast.
      "Fill in this form." → He said I had got to fill in this form.
